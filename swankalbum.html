This was an album cover design I did for an Atlanta based rock band named Swank Sinatra. Their album title was "Pretty Shoes"

This band does very high energy live performances with unexpected humarous acts. Their fans enjoy that about them. The design challenge here was to create a high energy cover that is rock n roll + humar that the band approves and their fans can resonate.

Sketches were developed through out brianstorming with the band, researching on relavent materials, etc. Once the design idea was formed, I digitized the sketch and started coloring

Various color combinations were offered. The band chose the orange-red version - which is also my favorite!

My Role Here: Ideation, Design, Art Direction, Branding
